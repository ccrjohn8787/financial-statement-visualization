Key Insights

✓ Pricing Power Intact
Gross margin improved to 45.2% (up from 43.1% YoY). This indicates Apple can still raise prices and customers will pay - similar to luxury brands like Rolex where brand value exceeds production cost.
! China Revenue Declining
Greater China revenue down 8% YoY ($20.8B → $19.1B). This is Apple's second-largest market showing weakness for third consecutive quarter. Represents 16% of total revenue at risk.
✓ Services Revenue Boom
Services hit record $22.3B (up 12% YoY) with 70% margins. This high-margin recurring revenue stream now represents 20% of total revenue, providing stability and predictability.
⚠ Valuation Stretched
Trading at 31.2x earnings vs 5-year average of 22x. Current valuation implies 15% annual growth but company delivering only 5%. May limit upside potential in near term.
